## What I studied

My master's studies at Tampere University focused on software engineering at a larger scale: software production, project management, process thinking, and information-based web systems.

The most useful part was connecting technical choices to the way software is planned, coordinated, delivered, and maintained. That made the studies a natural continuation of practical industry work instead of a separate academic track.

## Thesis work

The thesis was a chance to take one topic deep enough to be judged carefully and still keep it connected to practical engineering. Finishing it with the maximum grade made the effort visible, but the lasting value was the discipline of framing a problem, evaluating evidence, and writing the result clearly.
